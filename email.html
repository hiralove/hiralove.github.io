Create Account
If you want to enjoy AWeber autoresponder series service, you first need to create an account on the same. For this, you need to follow stated below points.

  • First you need to visit at the official website of AWeber.
  • Here, you need to click on “Register” option.
  •  By clicking on “Register” option, you will reach at a new page.
  • Here, you are required to enter certain information of you such as your full name, email address, contact number, etc.
  • You also need to choose a user name and password.
  • Once done, click on submit option.
  • Now, you may need to verify your email address and contact details.

Process to Create an Autoresponsder Series with AWeber

  • First of all, you need to log into your AWeber account.
  • Once you logged into your account, you need to click on MESSAGES option.
  • Now, choose the FOLLOW UP SERIES tab.
  • You will be redirected to a new page where you should click on “create a follow up button”.
  • Choose a right email builder option from given drop down menu.
  •  Here, you can easily draft an autoresponder series email.
  • Write an email.
  • Now, click on “next” option.
  • You will be redirected to a new webpage.
  • Here, you will find various settings for email campaign.
  • It is highly suggested to create a welcome email for your subscribers.
  • Now, you can click on “save and exit” option.
You will be taken to your home page where you can see your very first autoresponder email series.
